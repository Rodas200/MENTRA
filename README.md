Mentra is a full-stack MERN web application designed to bridge the gap between freshers and working professionals through a secure and easy-to-use job referral platform. The application allows fresh graduates and job seekers to create professional profiles, upload resumes, showcase their skills, and connect with experienced mentors from different companies and getting advices. Freshers can send referral requests directly to professionals for their desired job roles, while mentors can review profiles, evaluate resumes, and either accept referrals or provide constructive feedback through a "Needs Improvement" option. The platform also enables users to track the status of their referral requests in real time, making the referral process transparent and organized. Built with React.js, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, JWT Authentication, and Tailwind CSS, Mentra focuses on providing a modern, responsive, and secure user experience. The primary goal of the project is to simplify networking, increase referral opportunities, and help talented candidates connect with professionals who can guide them toward successful careers. Mentra aims to make job referrals more accessible, transparent, and beneficial for both freshers and mentors..
